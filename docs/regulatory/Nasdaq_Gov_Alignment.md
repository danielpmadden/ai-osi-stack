# Nasdaq Governance Alignment for AI Oversight

## Audit Committee Responsibilities for AI Risk Oversight
- Oversee integration of AI risk metrics into enterprise risk management (ERM) dashboards.
- Review quarterly AI control effectiveness reports and assurance findings.
- Monitor incident response readiness, including tabletop exercises and post-incident remediation tracking.
- Evaluate adequacy of disclosure controls relating to AI incidents and cyber events.
- Coordinate with Technology & Ethics Committee to avoid oversight gaps or duplication.

## Board Risk Oversight Mapping to OSI Layers
| OSI Layer | Board Oversight Focus | Committee Lead | Reporting Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 Mandate | Strategic alignment, risk appetite, charter compliance | Full Board | Annual review, ad hoc updates |
| 1 Ethics | Responsible AI principles, stakeholder impact assessments | Technology & Ethics Committee | Semi-annual briefings |
| 2 Data | Data rights, privacy compliance, vendor attestations | Audit & Risk Committee | Quarterly dashboards |
| 3 Model | Model selection, validation, robustness | Technology & Ethics Committee | Quarterly technical brief |
| 4 Instruction | Prompt governance, access controls | Audit & Risk Committee | Quarterly |
| 5 Reasoning | Human-in-the-loop efficacy, explainability | Technology & Ethics Committee | Semi-annual |
| 6 Deployment | Change management, release approvals | Audit & Risk Committee | Quarterly |
| 7 Publication | External disclosure controls, investor communications | Audit & Risk Committee with IR liaison | Quarterly |
| 8 Civic | Public policy alignment, societal commitments | Full Board | Annual strategy retreat |

## Internal Controls Over AI-Related Disclosure
- Disclosure committee integrates AI-specific risk assessments into standard financial reporting cycles.
- Controls emphasize early detection of material AI incidents, cross-functional review of narratives, and preservation of legal privilege.
- Custodian maintains mapping documents linking board oversight artifacts to disclosure requirements, enabling rapid assembly of supporting evidence for filings.
- Management performs quarterly certifications confirming no undisclosed AI incidents meet materiality thresholds.
