# Disclosure Language Safe Harbor Samples

> Illustrative only; consult counsel before use in any filing or public communication.

## Risk Factor Sample (Form 10-K / 20-F)
"Our deployment of artificial intelligence technologies exposes us to operational, ethical, and regulatory risks. AI systems rely on complex data inputs and third-party models that may contain errors, biases, or vulnerabilities. Despite our governance controls described in the AI OSI Stack framework, these measures may not prevent unauthorized access, misinformed decisions, or evolving regulatory obligations. Any significant failure could disrupt operations, harm our reputation, or subject us to legal and regulatory scrutiny."

## Governance & Risk Management Narrative (Annual Report)
"We maintain a custodial AI OSI Stack governance framework overseen by our Board’s Audit & Risk and Technology & Ethics Committees. Management’s AI Risk Council implements policies covering data stewardship, model validation, prompt governance, and incident response. Operational artifacts remain within our restricted Control Tower environment, while this public repository provides publication-grade documentation of control objectives, risk registers, and disclosure playbooks to support transparent oversight."

## Form 8-K Short-Form Incident Acknowledgement
"On [date], we identified an AI-related cybersecurity incident involving [general descriptor]. We activated our AI incident response protocol, contained the issue, and commenced remediation. We continue to assess the impact on our operations and stakeholders and will provide updates as appropriate."
