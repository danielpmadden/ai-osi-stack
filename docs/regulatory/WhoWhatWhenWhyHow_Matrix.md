# AI OSI Stack v5: Who / What / When / Why / How Matrix

## Governance Matrix
| Domain | WHO (Owner/Role) | WHAT (Artifacts/Evidence) | WHEN (Cadence/Triggers) | WHY (Risk/Control Objective) | HOW (Controls & Methods) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mandate | Board of Directors; Custodian | Board resolutions, charter addendum, strategic memos | Annual approval; triggered by major AI initiatives | Align AI investments with corporate mission and risk appetite | Governance briefings, risk appetite workshops, documented votes |
| Ethics | Technology & Ethics Committee; Ethics Lead | Responsible AI principles, stakeholder consultation summaries | Semi-annual review; triggered by new use cases | Prevent ethical harms and ensure stakeholder inclusion | Ethics impact assessments, scenario workshops, consent reviews |
| Data | Audit & Risk Committee; Data Steward | Data inventories, provenance attestations, privacy overlays | Quarterly; triggered by data sourcing changes | Protect data rights, privacy, and quality | Data due diligence checklists, privacy impact reviews, segregation of duties |
| Model | AI Risk Council; Model Validation Lead | Validation narratives, evaluation reports, bias testing results | Quarterly; pre-deployment | Ensure model robustness and fairness | Independent validation reviews, challenger models, documented thresholds |
| Instruction | Prompt Governance Team; Access Control Owner | Prompt libraries, access logs, usage guidelines | Quarterly; triggered by policy breach | Prevent misuse and prompt injection risks | Role-based access controls, prompt change approvals, monitoring of prompt outputs |
| Reasoning | Human Oversight Leads; CRO | Decision review matrices, explainability briefs | Semi-annual; triggered by complex deployments | Maintain interpretability and accountability | Human-in-the-loop checkpoints, explanation templates, escalation protocols |
| Deployment | Change Advisory Board; DevSecOps Liaison | Release readiness assessments, rollback plans | Every release; triggered by hotfixes | Prevent unstable deployments and ensure rollback capability | Segregated environments, deployment playbooks, sign-off checklists |
| Publication | Disclosure Committee; Investor Relations | Disclosure checklists, communication scripts | Quarterly; triggered by incidents or earnings | Maintain accurate, timely external communications | Cross-functional disclosure reviews, legal vetting, message testing |
| Civic | Public Policy Liaison; Corporate Responsibility | Civic engagement logs, transparency reports | Annual; triggered by regulatory consultations | Foster societal trust and regulatory cooperation | Policy roundtables, public comment responses, community feedback loops |

## Assurance Methods by Layer
| Layer | Review Type | Frequency | Outputs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mandate (0) | Board charter compliance review | Annual | Board minutes, compliance attestation |
| Ethics (1) | Ethical impact audit | Semi-annual | Findings summary, action plan |
| Data (2) | Data governance assurance | Quarterly | Data quality report, remediation log |
| Model (3) | Independent validation | Quarterly/pre-release | Validation memo, risk ratings |
| Instruction (4) | Access and prompt governance review | Quarterly | Access certification, prompt audit notes |
| Reasoning (5) | Human oversight effectiveness review | Semi-annual | Oversight scorecard, exception log |
| Deployment (6) | Release management audit | Each deployment cycle | Deployment checklist, rollback verification |
| Publication (7) | Disclosure control assessment | Quarterly | Disclosure readiness report |
| Civic (8) | Stakeholder engagement review | Annual | Engagement summary, policy recommendations |
