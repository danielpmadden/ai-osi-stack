# Training and Awareness Session for Board & Executives

## Session Overview
- **Duration**: 75 minutes (plus pre-read time).
- **Objective**: Equip directors and executives with decision-useful understanding of AI OSI Stack governance, risk posture, incident response, and disclosure expectations.
- **Format**: Live briefing with interactive discussion prompts and decision checkpoints.

## Agenda
| Time | Topic | Lead | Discussion Prompts | Decision Checkpoints |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0:00–0:10 | Welcome & Objectives | Board Chair | What outcomes do we expect from this session? | Confirm agenda and desired outputs. |
| 0:10–0:25 | Governance Structure & Roles | Custodian | How do committees coordinate oversight? | Validate RACI alignment, confirm charter addendum adoption. |
| 0:25–0:40 | Risk Landscape | CRO | Which risks exceed appetite? | Approve updates to risk appetite statements if needed. |
| 0:40–0:55 | Controls by OSI Layer | AI Risk Council Lead | Are controls sufficient at each layer? | Identify layers requiring assurance deep dives. |
| 0:55–1:05 | Incident Response & Disclosure | General Counsel | How do we meet 72-hour expectations? | Endorse communication triggers and escalation paths. |
| 1:05–1:15 | Third-Party & Shadow AI Oversight | Vendor Manager | Are vendors meeting governance standards? | Decide on any new certification requirements. |
| 1:15–1:20 | Action Items & Metrics | Board Chair | What metrics indicate progress before next meeting? | Assign owners and timelines for follow-ups. |

## Discussion Prompts (Supplemental)
- Which emerging regulations require immediate board attention?
- How will we measure success of shadow AI mitigation efforts?
- What cross-functional dependencies could delay incident response decisions?
- How do we ensure investor messaging remains consistent across channels?

## Follow-Up Actions & Metrics
- Track completion of remediation items from audit checklist reviews.
- Measure training attendance, comprehension quiz results, and policy acknowledgment rates.
- Monitor time-to-close for incident action items and third-party findings.
- Schedule quarterly refreshers focusing on evolving regulatory requirements.
- Report progress in board watchlist section of quarterly governance packet.
