## Key Terms
- **AI OSI Stack:** The layered governance blueprint translating civic intent into enforceable AI artefacts.
- **AEIP (Accountable Evidence Interchange Protocol):** The protocol suite that transports intents, justifications, counter-signatures, commitments, and updates between layers.
- **Integrity Ledger Entry:** A cryptographically sealed record of AEIP exchanges that SHALL be referenced by every deployment and governance publication.
- **Civic Mandate Ledger:** Registry storing authorization scope, consent, and renewal clauses defined in [Layer 0 – Civic Mandate](./04_Layer_0_Civic_Mandate.md).
- **Governance Control Tower:** FastAPI + SQLModel and React-based scaffold that SHOULD expose dashboards and API endpoints for registry synchronization.
- **Interpretive Canon:** Optional semantic extensions (19A–24) guiding persona design, therapy governance, and meta-oversight.
- **Deferred Signing Ceremony:** Ritualized sealing process ensuring custodial stewardship and provenance before production signatures are issued.
- **Persona Segregation:** Obligation in [Layer 3 – Model Development](./07_Layer_3_Model_Development.md) that SHALL maintain separation of roles and capabilities to honor ethical charters.
- **Renewal Directive:** Feedback signal from [Layer 8 – Civic Participation](./12_Layer_8_Civic_Participation.md) instructing mandate updates.
