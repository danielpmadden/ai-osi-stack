## Purpose
Layer 7 transforms internal evidence into public disclosures, oversight reports, and registry updates. It SHALL curate materials that affected communities, regulators, and investors can verify.

## Core Functions
- Aggregate sealed manifests from [Layer 6 – Deployment & Integration](./10_Layer_6_Deployment_Integration.md).
- Publish public disclosure dossiers and standards crosswalks for civic audiences.
- Supply governance publications to [Layer 8 – Civic Participation](./12_Layer_8_Civic_Participation.md) feedback channels.

## Dependencies
Requires AEIP receipts, assurance manifests, and Integrity Ledger references. Publications SHALL cite unique identifiers traceable back to [Layer 0](./04_Layer_0_Civic_Mandate.md) mandates.

## Required Artefacts

- **Standards Crosswalk Table:** SHALL map artefacts to ISO 42001, NIST AI RMF, and EU AI Act obligations.
- **Oversight Registry Update:** SHOULD record dissemination to regulators and civic councils.

## Governance Controls
- Publications SHALL undergo legal and civic review before release.
- All disclosures SHOULD be versioned and stored in the Governance Control Tower with AEIP verification metadata.
