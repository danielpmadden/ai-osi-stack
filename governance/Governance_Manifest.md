# Governance Manifest

## Editorial Authority
- Stewarded by the Civic Standards Council with rotating municipal representation.
- Editorial decisions SHALL require quorum endorsements recorded in the Integrity Ledger.

## Ratification Procedure
1. Publish proposed amendments with 30-day public comment period.
2. Convene oversight forum deliberations with Persona Architecture observers.
3. Ratify changes via supermajority vote; record AEIP attestations and SVC increments.

## Alignment with Civic Charter
- All mandates MUST uphold civic rights enumerated in the Civic Charter.
- Implementation guidance SHOULD reflect equitable participation, accessibility, and transparency.
- Emergency revisions MAY be expedited with public notice and subsequent retrospective review.
