# Governance Manifest

## Editorial Authority
- Stewarded by the Civic Standards Council with rotating municipal representation (Clauses L00-C001, L08-C003).
- Editorial decisions SHALL require quorum endorsements recorded in the Integrity Ledger (Clauses L00-C001, L11-C001).

## Ratification Procedure
1. Publish proposed amendments with 30-day public comment period (Clauses L07-C001, L07-C003).
2. Convene oversight forum deliberations with Persona Architecture observers (Clauses L05-C001, L05-C002).
3. Ratify changes via supermajority vote; record AEIP attestations and SVC increments (Clauses L04-C001, L09-C001, L11-C001).

## Alignment with Civic Charter
- All mandates MUST uphold civic rights enumerated in the Civic Charter (Clauses L02-C001, L06-C002, L09-C003).
- Implementation guidance SHOULD reflect equitable participation, accessibility, and transparency (Clauses L07-C002, L08-C002, L10-C003).
- Emergency revisions MAY be expedited with public notice and subsequent retrospective review (Clauses L06-C002, L12-C002, L12-C003).

Clause references in this manifest align with `reference/clause_index.yaml` to guarantee that editorial processes invoke the appropriate AEIP evidence pathways.
