# Civic Charter

## Civic Legitimacy
- Affirms that the AI OSI Stack operates under civic oversight and public accountability.
- Recognizes communities as co-authors of governance obligations and rights.

## Rights and Protections
- Individuals SHALL retain agency over personal data and civic representations.
- Communities MUST receive transparent reporting and accessible appeals pathways.
- Public archives SHOULD preserve deliberative records for generational stewardship.

## Participatory Renewal
- Charter reviews SHALL occur every eighteen months with open assembly sessions.
- Ratified updates MUST be logged through AEIP messages and Integrity Ledger entries.
- Civic partners MAY propose amendments through recognized oversight channels.
