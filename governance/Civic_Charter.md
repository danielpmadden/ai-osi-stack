# Civic Charter

## Civic Legitimacy
- Affirms that the AI OSI Stack operates under civic oversight and public accountability (Clauses L00-C001, L04-C001, L06-C001).
- Recognizes communities as co-authors of governance obligations and rights (Clauses L03-C002, L08-C001, L08-C003).

## Rights and Protections
- Individuals SHALL retain agency over personal data and civic representations (Clauses L02-C001, L05-C002, L06-C002).
- Communities MUST receive transparent reporting and accessible appeals pathways (Clauses L00-C002, L02-C003, L07-C001).
- Public archives SHOULD preserve deliberative records for generational stewardship (Clauses L05-C001, L07-C002, L09-C001).

## Participatory Renewal
- Charter reviews SHALL occur every eighteen months with open assembly sessions (Clauses L00-C003, L08-C001, L08-C002).
- Ratified updates MUST be logged through AEIP messages and Integrity Ledger entries (Clauses L00-C002, L07-C003, L11-C001).
- Civic partners MAY propose amendments through recognized oversight channels (Clauses L03-C001, L09-C003, L10-C003).

Clause identifiers cited in this charter resolve to normative entries in `reference/clause_index.yaml`, ensuring every civic right, duty, and AEIP requirement is anchored to a verifiable governance clause.
