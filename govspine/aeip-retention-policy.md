# AEIP Archival Retention Policy

- All AEIP frames must be retained for a minimum of five years.
- Frames older than five years may be transferred to cold storage but must remain discoverable through the Control Tower API.
- Deletions require dual approval recorded in `govspine/control/change-control-records/`.
