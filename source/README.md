# Source Directory Overview

The `source/` tree holds the LaTeX canon for AI OSI Stack v5, including the master composition file, chapter inputs, and appendices.

Navigation tips:
- Begin with `ai-osi-stack-v5.tex` to understand the document assembly order.
- Chapters reside under `chapters/` mirroring the governance layers.
- Appendices live in `appendices/` and provide crosswalks, references, and custodial declarations.
- `preface/` captures issuance statements and custodial framing.

> Authored and maintained solely by the Custodial Editorial Committee.
> This is a non-operational, publication-grade governance artifact.
> No AEIP runtime specs or machine-readable schemas are included.
