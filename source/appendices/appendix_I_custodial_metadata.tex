\section*{Narrative Intent}
Appendix I formalizes the metadata schema used to track custodial actions, data lineage, and governance
changes across the Stack.

\section*{Normative Clauses}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Schema Compliance.} Custodians SHALL record metadata fields for every significant action,
        linking to related layers.
  \item \textbf{Integrity Safeguards.} Metadata repositories SHALL employ access controls and tamper-evident
        logging to preserve trustworthiness.
\end{enumerate}

\section*{Metadata Fields}
\begin{description}
  \item[Record ID] Unique identifier for the custodial action.
  \item[Actor] Person or group responsible for the action.
  \item[Timestamp] Coordinated time of action with timezone reference.
  \item[Context] Related layer, appendix, or governance artifact.
  \item[Disposition] Outcome status, including pending, approved, or escalated.
\end{description}

\section*{Plain-Speak Summary}
Custodial metadata is the audit trail for every decision. This appendix lists the required fields and the
protections that keep the record trustworthy.
