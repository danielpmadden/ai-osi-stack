# Chapter 19A — Usage and Trust Audit

## Strengths
- Policy lens: Connects usage covenants to enforceable incident reporting, reinforcing accountability for frontline deployments.
- Enterprise lens: Provides straightforward workflow for documenting exceptions and restorative actions.
- Legal/Ethical lens: Emphasises trust ledgers that protect communities from opaque operational changes.

## Gaps
- Policy: Should mention consent renewal expectations when usage conditions evolve.
- Enterprise: Needs staffing guidance for maintaining public trust dashboards.
- Legal/Ethical: Could clarify remedy timelines after breach acknowledgment.

## Recommended clarifications or additional clauses
- Clarify who verifies TECL submissions before activation.
- Add guidance on public reporting cadence for ledger updates.
