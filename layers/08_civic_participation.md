# Layer 08 — Civic Participation

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for civic participation. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L08-C001 — Assembly Scheduling Transparency
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer08.participation.schedule

Public assemblies SHALL publish agendas and schedules through AEIP notices referencing Clause L08-C001 to ensure equitable civic access.

### Clause L08-C002 — Participation Accessibility Support
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer08.participation.accessSupport

Accessibility accommodations (interpretation, stipends, childcare) MUST be documented in AEIP envelopes citing Clause L08-C002 before each assembly to confirm rights-aligned participation.

### Clause L08-C003 — Feedback Incorporation Logs
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer08.participation.feedback

Custodial teams SHALL publish feedback incorporation logs after participatory events, using AEIP payloads referencing Clause L08-C003 to demonstrate renewal responsiveness.
