# Layer 02 — Data Stewardship

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for data stewardship. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L02-C001 — Civic Data Provenance
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer02.data.provenance

All civic data ingested into the stack MUST carry provenance metadata attested via AEIP envelopes referencing Clause L02-C001 to safeguard individual agency.

### Clause L02-C002 — Community Consent Renewal
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer02.data.consent

Consent artifacts for community datasets SHALL be renewed at least annually, with AEIP notifications citing Clause L02-C002 to document re-authorization outcomes.

### Clause L02-C003 — Stewardship Escalation Window
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer02.data.escalation

Custodial stewards MUST provide a ten-day escalation window for data handling disputes, logging each escalation envelope with Clause L02-C003 to confirm availability of civic recourse.
