# Layer 04 — Instruction Control

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for instruction control. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L04-C001 — Instruction Policy Registry
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer04.control.policyRegistry

Instruction policy updates SHALL be ratified through the Persona Architecture oversight loop, and every update MUST be logged with AEIP envelopes referencing Clause L04-C001 to maintain civic accountability.

### Clause L04-C002 — Real-time Safeguard Overrides
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer04.control.safeguardOverride

Override events affecting protected classes MUST capture justification, duration, and review outcomes. AEIP signals citing Clause L04-C002 SHALL be issued within five minutes of activation to protect civic rights.

### Clause L04-C003 — Instruction Red Team Feedback
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer04.control.redTeam

Community-aligned red team exercises SHALL occur quarterly, with findings documented via AEIP payloads referencing Clause L04-C003 to embed participatory renewal into control tuning.
