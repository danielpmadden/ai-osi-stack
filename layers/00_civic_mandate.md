# Layer 00 — Civic Mandate

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for civic mandate. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L00-C001 — Civic Oversight Quorum
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer00.mandate.quorum

Municipal oversight councils SHALL maintain a publicly documented quorum roster and record every quorum validation through AEIP envelopes that cite Clause L00-C001.

### Clause L00-C002 — Transparency Broadcast
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer00.mandate.transparency

Custodial stewards MUST publish quarterly transparency briefs summarizing mandate execution. Each brief SHALL include an AEIP payload referencing Clause L00-C002 to guarantee archival accessibility.

### Clause L00-C003 — Renewal Notice Period
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer00.mandate.renewalNotice

The Civic Standards Council SHALL issue renewal notices no later than ninety days before each charter review. Notices MUST be transmitted via AEIP envelopes logged against Clause L00-C003 for traceable participatory scheduling.
