# Layer 11 — Implementation Verification

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for implementation verification. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L11-C001 — Verification Evidence Bundles
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer11.verification.evidence

Verification reports SHALL include evidence bundles linked to clause attestations. AEIP transmissions referencing Clause L11-C001 MUST enumerate tests executed and civic observers engaged.

### Clause L11-C002 — Nonconformity Correction Windows
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer11.verification.corrections

Identified nonconformities impacting rights MUST be corrected within fifteen days, with AEIP updates citing Clause L11-C002 to document remediation progress.

### Clause L11-C003 — Community Verification Briefings
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer11.verification.briefings

Community verification briefings SHALL be convened after each major audit cycle, logging AEIP envelopes referencing Clause L11-C003 to affirm participatory oversight.
