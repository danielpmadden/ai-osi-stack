# Layer 09 — Epistemic Infrastructure Protocol

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for epistemic infrastructure protocol. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L09-C001 — Evidence Graph Publication
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer09.epistemic.evidenceGraph

Evidence graphs supporting civic decisions SHALL be published within seven days of issuance. AEIP attestations referencing Clause L09-C001 MUST include resolvable graph pointers.

### Clause L09-C002 — Terminology Drift Alerts
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer09.epistemic.drift

Semantic drift in core terms SHALL trigger AEIP alerts citing Clause L09-C002, outlining remediation steps and inviting public comment.

### Clause L09-C003 — Knowledge Steward Escalation
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer09.epistemic.stewardEscalation

Knowledge stewards MUST provide escalation channels for challenges to epistemic sources. Each escalation SHALL log an AEIP envelope referencing Clause L09-C003 to uphold accountability protections.
