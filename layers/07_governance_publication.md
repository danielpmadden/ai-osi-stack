# Layer 07 — Governance & Publication

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for governance & publication. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L07-C001 — Publication Window Guarantees
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer07.publication.window

Governance publications affecting civic rights MUST be released at least fourteen days before enforcement. AEIP notices referencing Clause L07-C001 SHALL certify compliance with the publication window.

### Clause L07-C002 — Accessibility Format Coverage
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer07.publication.accessibility

All canonical publications SHALL include accessible formats (screen reader, low-bandwidth, multilingual) logged via AEIP envelopes citing Clause L07-C002 to demonstrate inclusive legitimacy.

### Clause L07-C003 — Comment Digest Distribution
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer07.publication.commentDigest

Public comment periods MUST end with a digest summarizing feedback themes, recorded with AEIP payloads referencing Clause L07-C003 to honor participatory renewal commitments.
