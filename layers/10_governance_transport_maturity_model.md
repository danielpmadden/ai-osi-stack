# Layer 10 — Governance Transport Maturity Model

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for governance transport maturity model. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L10-C001 — Transport Capability Baselines
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer10.transport.baseline

Governance transport capabilities SHALL be benchmarked annually with AEIP envelopes referencing Clause L10-C001 to certify maturity scores and civic readiness.

### Clause L10-C002 — Interchange Safeguard Testing
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer10.transport.safeguards

Interchange nodes handling personal data MUST pass quarterly safeguard tests. Results SHALL be distributed via AEIP payloads citing Clause L10-C002 to protect rights across transport layers.

### Clause L10-C003 — Civic Partner Onboarding Support
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer10.transport.partnerOnboarding

New civic partners SHALL receive onboarding kits documenting transport expectations, logged through AEIP references to Clause L10-C003 to confirm inclusive maturity scaling.
