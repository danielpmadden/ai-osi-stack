# Layer 05 — Reasoning Exchange

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for reasoning exchange. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L05-C001 — Deliberation Transcript Fidelity
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer05.reasoning.transcripts

Reasoning exchanges with civic stakeholders SHALL capture verbatim transcripts. AEIP submissions referencing Clause L05-C001 MUST certify transcript integrity and availability to public archives.

### Clause L05-C002 — Persona Hand-off Logging
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer05.reasoning.personaHandOff

When persona roles shift during live deliberations, the orchestrator MUST log context continuity via AEIP envelopes citing Clause L05-C002 to ensure protections follow affected communities.

### Clause L05-C003 — Reflection Cycle Cadence
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer05.reasoning.reflection

Structured reflection cycles SHALL occur after every major release, with learnings distributed through AEIP payloads referencing Clause L05-C003 to invite civic feedback on reasoning quality.
