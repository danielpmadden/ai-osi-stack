# Layer 06 — Deployment Integration

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for deployment integration. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L06-C001 — Deployment Gate Reviews
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer06.deployment.gateReview

Every deployment SHALL pass a gate review that includes civic observer sign-off. AEIP envelopes citing Clause L06-C001 MUST document approval artifacts and dissent notes.

### Clause L06-C002 — Rollback Transparency
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer06.deployment.rollback

Rollback decisions impacting public services MUST include transparency notices issued through AEIP payloads referencing Clause L06-C002 to protect community access rights.

### Clause L06-C003 — Integration Sandbox Reporting
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer06.deployment.sandbox

Integration sandboxes SHALL publish monthly experiment summaries, logged with Clause L06-C003 to invite community prototyping feedback before production adoption.
