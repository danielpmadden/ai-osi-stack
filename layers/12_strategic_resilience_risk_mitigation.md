# Layer 12 — Strategic Resilience & Risk Mitigation

## Overview
This section outlines the normative controls and requirements for strategic resilience & risk mitigation. Detailed clauses will be developed in alignment with Version 5 governance directives.

## Key Objectives
- Establish measurable obligations.
- Maintain alignment with the civic mandate.
- Provide traceability to the v4→v5 crosswalk.

## Normative Clauses

### Clause L12-C001 — Resilience Scenario Catalog
**Charter Reference:** Civic Legitimacy  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer12.resilience.scenarios

Resilience planning SHALL maintain a catalog of civic-impact scenarios. AEIP readiness attestations citing Clause L12-C001 MUST document scenario coverage and responsible leads.

### Clause L12-C002 — Rapid Response Accountability
**Charter Reference:** Rights and Protections  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer12.resilience.response

Rapid response activations MUST record decision timelines, affected populations, and protective measures via AEIP envelopes referencing Clause L12-C002 to uphold rights during emergencies.

### Clause L12-C003 — Adaptive Learning Cycles
**Charter Reference:** Participatory Renewal  
**AEIP Pointer:** aeip.layer12.resilience.learning

Resilience teams SHALL conduct adaptive learning cycles after each incident, publishing AEIP payloads referencing Clause L12-C003 to share lessons with civic partners.
