# AI OSI Stack Manifesto (Version 5)

## 0.0 Foreword
This manifesto codifies the mission system, normative principles, and governance obligations for the AI OSI Stack Version 5 (Open-Core) canonical edition. It is written in an ISO-style structure to provide traceability and audit readiness.

## 1.0 Purpose
The AI OSI Stack exists to articulate a civic-resilient framework for responsible artificial intelligence infrastructure. Version 5 prioritizes provable accountability, participatory governance, and transparent lineage across the AI lifecycle, ensuring that each layer can be certified against public obligations.

## 2.0 Scope
This manifesto applies to all artifacts, releases, and derivative operational guidance produced under the AI OSI Stack Version 5 canonical specification. It governs the design, stewardship, publication, and verification practices for layers 00–12 and associated appendices.

## 3.0 Terms and Definitions
- **AI OSI Stack** — The layered specification describing the civic-operational interface for AI systems.
- **Canonical Edition** — The normative release designated for public reference and compliance assessment.
- **Custodian** — The designated steward responsible for maintaining archival integrity and public availability of the specification.

## 4.0 Governance Commitments
4.1 The custodian shall maintain transparent change control, including public changelog updates for each ratified revision.
4.2 Governance proceedings shall document stakeholder participation, consent thresholds, and escalation channels for dispute resolution.
4.3 Public verification routines shall remain openly accessible to enable independent validation of compliance claims.

## 5.0 Stewardship Obligations
5.1 Preserve the Version 4 source materials for reference while clearly superseding them with Version 5 directives.
5.2 Publish all normative updates under CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 International and maintain provenance metadata.
5.3 Facilitate civic participation through structured comment periods, crosswalk transparency, and forward roadmaps.
