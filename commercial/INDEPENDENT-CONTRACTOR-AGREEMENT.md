## 2. Purpose
Principal engages Contractor to support delivery of services related to AI OSI Stack pilots, consulting, workshops, or advisory retainers as described in applicable task orders or Statements of Work (each a "Work Order"). ## 3. Scope of Services
3.1 Contractor shall perform the services set forth in each Work Order, which will describe deliverables, timelines, and compensation.

3.3 Contractor shall not make commitments or representations on behalf of Principal without written authorization.
3.4 All services are delivered remotely unless travel is explicitly approved and funded by Principal or Client in advance.

## 4. Term and Termination
4.1 This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.  
4.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any Work Order for convenience upon seven (7) days written notice. Principal will compensate Contractor for services performed through the termination date.  
4.3 Either Party may terminate immediately for material breach if not cured within seven (7) days of written notice.

## 5. Compensation and Expenses
5.1 Compensation will be set forth in each Work Order, aligned with the applicable Rate Card.  
5.2 Unless otherwise agreed, Contractor shall invoice monthly. Payment terms are Net 15 from receipt of invoice.  
5.3 Contractor is responsible for all taxes and benefits associated with the compensation received.
5.4 Pre-approved travel, lodging, per diem, and related expenses are reimbursable at cost with receipts. Travel days may be billed at fifty percent (50%) of the agreed daily rate.
5.5 No other benefits, insurance, or retirement contributions are provided by Principal.
5.6 Initial deposits are non-refundable and secure scheduling and resource allocation for the applicable Work Order.
5.7 Principal may suspend Services or reassign Work Order commitments if any Contractor invoice remains unpaid for more than fifteen (15) days past the agreed due date, with schedules rebooked upon payment restoration.
5.8 For retainers extending beyond three (3) months, compensation is subject to a cost-of-living adjustment of three percent (3%) per quarter applied prospectively.
