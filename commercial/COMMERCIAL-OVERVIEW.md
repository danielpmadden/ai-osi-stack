## Executive Summary
AI OSI Stack is a civic-minded governance and assurance framework for responsible AI operations. Conceived, authored, and maintained by Daniel P. Madden as an independent, self-funded project, the platform delivers modular tooling, policy schemas, and playbooks for institutions seeking transparent, accountable AI. There is no institutional, corporate, or governmental backing.
